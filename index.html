Together, we can end homelessness.

The goal of Project Spot is lofty, but certainly achievable: to end homelessness in the Palm Beach County area. Learn how you can aid us in this goal with three simple steps.

  • Spot
  • Give
  • Share

1. Spot.

When you are out and about, take note of any homeless people that you see.

2. Give.

Use the extremely user-friendly Project Spot app to donate to the Palm Beach County Homeless Coalition every time you see a homeless person.

3. Share.

Spread the word about Project Spot.

That's it.

Why is this important?

Please consider these facts about the homeless in Palm Beach County.

  • Families

    There are over 1400 homeless families in Palm Beach County alone.

  • Children

    The average age of a homeless child in Palm Beach county is just seven years old.

  • Students

    More than 3000 students attend school without having a house to go home to.

  • Housing Prices

    The average household rent in Florida requires 2.4 full-time minimum wage earners working every week of the year.

  • Causes

    The major causes of homelessness include the poverty rate, lack of affordable housing, racial disparities, challenges of single parenting, mental illness, and traumatic experiences like domestic violence and child abuse.

  • LGBT

    Many homeless LGBT individuals face great difficulty and discrimination in finding shelters that accept and respect them; they are often at a heightened risk of violence, abuse, and exploitation.
